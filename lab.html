The Lord's Labyrinth is a vital part of your Path of Exile journey. The Lab allows you to pick an Ascendancy or sub-class.

Each starting class has 3 Ascendancies to choose from. Ascendancies are one of the first thing to look at when planning a build due to their strengths. While leveling your first character of each League, you will encounter Lab Trials. Once you complete the trials for each difficulty of the Lab you caan then take you attempt at defeating it.
